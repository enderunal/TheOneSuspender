Privacy Policy for The One Suspender

This extension, The One Suspender, is designed with your privacy in mind. It is an open-source project and operates entirely locally on your browser.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing information.

All settings and extension-related data are stored locally on your computer using the browser's storage mechanisms and are not sent to any external servers.

Since no data is collected, there is no data to share with third parties.

The source code is publicly available for review to ensure transparency.

If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, please feel free to open an issue on our GitHub issues page.
